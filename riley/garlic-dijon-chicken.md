
# Garlic Dijon Chicken

#### Ingredients

 - 2 chicken breasts
 - 2 tbsp minced garlic
 - 2 tbsp parsley
 - 2 tbsp lemon juice
 - 1 tbsp dijon mustard
 - 1/2 tbsp salt
 - 1 tsp pepper

 #### Directions

 1. mix all ingredients and marinade overnight.
 2. bake on skillet at 400 degrees farenheit for 25 minutes.
 3. broil for 5 minutes.
